# LIGO result handling decision

## Decision

No new LIGO training or independent-test inference is included in the present resubmission experiment scope. The ET SIS/PM independent evaluation is the quantitative headline analysis.

## Reason

The historical LIGO CQT/SEMD evaluation included training samples, and the historical checkpoints needed for a clean paired reevaluation are no longer available. Those numerical tables must not remain as formal independent-test evidence. Reconstructing the full LIGO protocol would expand the scope without addressing the central referee requests as efficiently as the completed ET calibration, selection-function, and physical-diagnostic analyses.

## Permitted manuscript treatment

Either remove the historical LIGO quantitative table entirely, or retain only a clearly labelled exploratory detector-domain-shift observation in Discussion/Appendix. Any retained validation AUC must be explicitly described as validation-derived, protocol-incomparable with the locked 0228 results, and not used in the abstract, conclusions, or formal model comparison.

The contribution statement should be limited to cross-lens-model transfer plus exploratory PSD-shift observations; it must not claim independently validated detector-domain robustness.
